Neil Armstrong

  • 1930 - Born in Wapakoneta, Ohio, USA, on August 5th
  • 1936 - Takes his first plane ride
  • 1946 - Earns his pilot's license, at the age of 16
  • 1930 - Joins the US Navy and takes part in the Korean War
  • 1952 - Joins the National Advisory Committee for Aeronautics (NACA), which will later become the National Aeronautics and Space Administration (NASA)
  • 1955 - Graduates with a Bachelor of Science degree in Aeronautical Engineering, at Purdue University, Indiana
  • 1956 - Marries to Janet Shearon
  • 1966 - Makes his first space flight as command astronaut of Gemini 8
  • 1969 - Becomes the first man on the Moon during the Apollo 11 mission
  • 1971 - Leaves NASA to become professor at the University of Cincinnati, Ohio
  • 2012 - Dies in Cincinnati, Ohio, USA, at the age of 82

"Neil was among the greatest of American heroes - not just of his time, but of all time."

- President Obama's Statement on Neil Armstrong's Death
